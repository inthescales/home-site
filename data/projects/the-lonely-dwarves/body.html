The Lonely Dwarves is an RPG video game for iOS. Join four brave dwarves as they undertake a perilous journey to defeat the evil threatening their world.

Features:

  • Classic turn based combat gameplay
  • Hand drawn pixel art
  • Crude jokes
Download The Lonely Dwarves here on the Apple app store.

The Lonely Dwarves was created in collaboration with Spazcosoft. I contributed the game's pixel artwork.

You can keep up with The Lonely Dwarves and other Spazcosoft projects on their Twitter account.
